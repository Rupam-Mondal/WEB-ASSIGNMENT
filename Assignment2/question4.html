• html tag: The root element that encapsulates the entire HTML document.
  • head tag: Contains metadata about the document, such as the document's title, character
  • p tag: Represents a paragraph of text. Used for grouping and formatting blocks of text.
  • h tag:Headings of various levels, with being the highest (most important) and the lowest. Used for structuring content and indicating hierarchy.
  • a: Creates hyperlinks to other web pages or resources. The href attribute specifies the link's destination.
