Features of HTML5:-

1.Semantics tags- <header> , <main> , <footer> , <article> these tags help to enhance the readability and also makes easier to brows for a search engine.

2.SVG support- HTML5 gives us the functionality to use svg nowadays.

3.audio and video tag- we can use audio and video tag in out html5.

4.APIs:- APIs are also included in HTML5 that gives more enhancement to developers.

5.Drag and drops- HTML5 supports drag and drop. It is very usefull when user is trying to upload some file
